01 — Overview

Making restaurant ordering simple

InstantMenu is a digital ordering platform that allows restaurant customers to browse menus and place orders by scanning a QR code—no app download required.

The platform supports dine-in, takeaway, delivery, drive-thru, and room service orders. It integrates with restaurant printers and payment systems, helping owners manage orders efficiently while giving customers a fast, contactless experience.

02 — Platforms

Mobile app and web experience

Android Mobile App: Restaurant owners use the native Android app to manage menus, track orders, and update availability in real time. The app connects directly to thermal printers commonly used in restaurants.

Web Application: A web-based version of the restaurant management platform built with Flutter Web, allowing owners to manage their restaurant from any browser without installing the mobile app.

Customer Ordering Interface: When customers scan a QR code at the restaurant, they access a streamlined web interface to browse menus, place orders, and track order status—no app download required.

03 — Key Features

What makes it work

QR Code Menu Access

Customers scan a QR code and instantly see the full menu. No app installation, no registration—just scan and browse.

Live Order Tracking

Customers can see their order status in real time, from placement to preparation to delivery.

Multiple Order Types

Supports dine-in, takeaway, delivery, drive-thru, and room service orders. Restaurants can enable any combination based on their operations.

Direct Payment Processing

Integrated payment gateways allow customers to pay online.

Custom QR Designs

Restaurants can customize their QR code designs to match their brand and display them on tables, counters, or promotional materials.

04 — Real-World Usage

Actively used by restaurants

InstantMenu has been downloaded over 10,000 times on Google Play and is actively used by restaurants for daily operations. The platform handles real customer orders across multiple service types.

Restaurants use the platform to reduce paper waste, speed up order processing, and offer customers a modern ordering experience. The system integrates with their existing hardware and payment infrastructure, making adoption straightforward.
