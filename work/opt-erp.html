01 ‚Äî The Problem

ERPs that don't go where work happens

Traditional ERP systems are locked to desktop browsers or specific operating systems. Business owners who need to check inventory, approve purchases, or review reports while away from their desk are left without access to critical data.

Building separate native apps for each platform (Android, iOS, Windows, macOS) is prohibitively expensive. Web-based ERPs sacrifice speed and offline capability for accessibility. The market needed a truly native, cross-platform solution.

02 ‚Äî The Solution

One codebase, four platforms, native performance

Opt ERP leverages Flutter's multi-platform capabilities to deliver a single, unified ERP experience across Android phones, iPads, Windows workstations, and macOS laptops. The UI adapts intelligently to each screen size and platform conventions.

The Laravel backend provides robust API endpoints for business logic, user management, and reporting ‚Äî while MySQL handles the structured data that ERP systems demand. The architecture separates concerns cleanly: Flutter handles presentation and local state, Laravel handles business rules and persistence.
