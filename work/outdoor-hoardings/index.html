Outdoor Hoardings
Advertisement System

An advertisement management platform with automated PPT, PDF, and Excel generation for outdoor hoarding campaigns.

Platform Web Application
Focus Automation & Document Generation
Role System Designer
01 — The Problem

Manual reporting kills ad operations

Outdoor advertising companies manage hundreds of hoarding locations across cities. Tracking which sites are booked, generating client proposals, creating billing documents, and producing campaign reports involves repetitive manual work — Excel sheets, PowerPoint presentations, and PDFs created from scratch for every client.

This manual process is slow, error-prone, and doesn't scale. Sales teams wait for operations to produce proposals. Clients wait for reports. Everyone loses time on mechanics instead of strategy.

02 — The Solution

Data in, documents out — automatically

The system centralizes all hoarding inventory, booking data, and client information into a single database. From this data, it automatically generates client-ready documents — proposals as PPTs, invoices as PDFs, and data exports as Excel sheets.

What previously took hours of manual document creation now happens in seconds. Sales teams generate proposals on the spot. Campaign reports are available the moment a campaign ends.

03 — Key Features

Automation at every step

PPT Generation

Automated PowerPoint proposals with hoarding photos, locations, pricing, and availability — branded and client-ready.

PDF Reports & Invoices

Campaign reports and billing documents generated from live data — no manual formatting or copy-paste.

Excel Data Exports

Structured data exports for inventory tracking, financial analysis, and custom reporting needs.

Inventory Management

Centralized hoarding inventory with location, size, availability, and pricing — searchable and filterable.
