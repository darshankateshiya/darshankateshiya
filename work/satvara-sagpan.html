01 ‚Äî The Problem

A community without a digital bridge

The Satvara community ‚Äî spread across Gujarat and beyond ‚Äî relied entirely on word-of-mouth, local gatherings, and paper-based directories for matrimony matchmaking. Families had no centralized way to discover profiles, verify details, or connect safely.

Existing generic matrimony apps didn't cater to the community's cultural nuances, language preferences (Gujarati), or specific requirements. The gap between demand and available digital solutions was widening.

02 ‚Äî The Solution

A platform built around community identity

Satvara Sagpan was designed as a culturally-aware matrimony platform ‚Äî not another generic clone. Every design decision, from language options to profile fields, was made with direct input from community members.

The architecture supports multi-platform access (Android, iOS, Web), ensuring reach across all demographics ‚Äî from younger, smartphone-native users to older family members browsing on desktops.

03 ‚Äî Key Features

What makes it work

Multi-Language Support

Full Gujarati and English language support throughout the app ‚Äî not just translation, but culturally adapted content and UI flowing naturally in both languages.

Apple Pay & PhonePe

Integrated payment systems supporting both Apple Pay for iOS users and PhonePe for Indian Android users ‚Äî covering the full demographic spectrum.

Push Notifications

Real-time push notifications for new match suggestions, profile views, and interest expressions ‚Äî keeping engagement high without being intrusive.

Advanced Profile Matching

Community-specific matching criteria beyond basic demographics ‚Äî incorporating cultural values, family background, and community-specific preferences.

04 ‚Äî System & Architecture

Built for reliability at scale

The system uses a Node.js REST API backend deployed on AWS, with MySQL for structured data storage. The Flutter frontend shares a single codebase across Android, iOS, and Web ‚Äî reducing maintenance overhead and ensuring consistent behavior.

Payment processing is handled via dedicated gateway integrations ‚Äî Apple Pay through Stripe for iOS, and PhonePe for Android UPI. Push notification delivery uses Firebase Cloud Messaging with topic-based targeting.
